Steak

How to Prepare and Cook a Steak.

The Cut
Choose Wisely.
  1. Steak must have been stored properly
  2. Try to get grass fed if possible
  3. Make sure the Steak is cut the way you like
  4. Should have a good amount of fat
  5. If you find good ones grab as many as you can!

Choosing a good piece of Steak is essential to the process of preparing and cooking a quailty Steak.

Temp to Remove Steak
Not so fast but also not to slow.

Rushing to pull the Steak off too soon may not be the best option because the meat hasn't fully cooked and it may get you some dirty look. On the other hand waiting too long to remove your Steak may lead to a trip to the dentist plus it wouldn't taste as good. Perfect temp is around 130-135 degrees which leaves you around medium-rare.

Let it Rest and Enjoy
I know your mouth is watering.

Please let your Steak rest and give it time to lock in all of those juices. Cutting into it too soon can cause a lot of flavor loss and make a bloody mess. Wait about 5 minutes before slicing in and you'll love yourself for it I promise.
